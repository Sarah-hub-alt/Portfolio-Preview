Sarah in a nutshell

I am a Kenyan web developer with a passion for building clean, accessible, and responsive websites. My interest in technology began with a curiosity about how digital platforms work behind the scenes, and over time that curiosity grew into a commitment to mastering the craft of web development. I enjoy experimenting with code, learning new frameworks, and exploring creative ways to turn ideas into interactive experiences.
Beyond my technical journey, I draw inspiration from photography, which sharpens my ability to notice detail and composition—qualities I carry into my design and coding work. I also enjoy reading novels, as stories give me fresh perspectives, broaden my imagination, and encourage me to think critically. These hobbies complement my professional life by shaping how I approach problem-solving and creativity.
My goal as a developer is not only to improve my skills but also to contribute to meaningful projects that have a real impact on people. I am enthusiastic about collaborating with others, sharing knowledge, and continually growing in this ever-changing field.
For me, web development is more than just code—it is an opportunity to create solutions that are functional, engaging, and purposeful.
